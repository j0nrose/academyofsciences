About Our Mission

The California Academy of Sciences is a world-class research institution and natural history museum dedicated to exploring, explaining, and sustaining life on Earth. Founded in 1853, we are one of the largest museums of natural history in the world.

Our scientists work on the frontlines of discovery, conducting groundbreaking research in biodiversity, evolution, and conservation. Through our exhibits, educational programs, and public outreach, we inspire millions of visitors annually to become engaged stewards of our planet.
